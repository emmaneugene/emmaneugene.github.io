about me:|

Hello! My name is Emmanuel Oh.

I am a final year Computer Science student wih a second major in Public Policy at Singapore Management University

I love solving problems, learning how things work under the hood, and recombining knowledge to create new ideas. I believe that my skills in software, DIY and working with people put me in good stead to make a dent in solving some of the wicked problems we face. I am especially interested in building reliable software that serves the public good or tackles pertinent issues relating to climate change.

My experience lies mainly with low-level and systems programming languages like C and Java, but I am also fluent with interpreted languages like Python and JavaScript. I have worked with both relational and document databases and have experience with cloud deployments (AWS, DigitalOcean, Google Cloud) and devops tooling (Docker, Gitlab, CloudFormation, Kubernetes). I am currently spending my time on full-stack web development, functional programming, and grokking the linux kernel.
